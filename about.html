About Us

Follow The Lacrosse is a media project created by lacrosse enthusiasts to centralise and amplify everything related to lacrosse in Europe. Our mission is to provide timely news, analysis, interviews and tournament information to support players, clubs, referees, coaches and organisers.

Our Mission

We believe that lacrosse has huge potential in Europe. By creating an accessible hub for news and resources, we aim to grow the sport’s popularity, connect communities and celebrate the achievements of everyone involved.
