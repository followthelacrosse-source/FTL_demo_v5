About This Tournament

Welcome to the Berlin Cup 2026, one of Europe’s premier field lacrosse events. Teams from across the continent will compete over three days in the heart of Germany’s capital. Join us for high‑level competition, camaraderie and the vibrant atmosphere of Berlin in spring.
